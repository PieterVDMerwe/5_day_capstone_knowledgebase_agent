# Kaelen Stormbringer

Kaelen Stormbringer is a figure defined by age and experience, an Elf veteran whose ninety-five years have etched wisdom onto his strong frame. Possessing a stocky and muscular build, Kaelen embodies the resilience of those who have survived countless conflicts.

His personality is a potent blend of traits honed by time: he is profoundly Cunning in his dealings, Loyal to a fault to those he deems worthy, and Wise in navigating the treacherous currents of fate. As a dedicated member of [[The Bloodrunners]], Kaelen carries the weight of their history and serves as an old veteran whose counsel is often sought.

Currently, Kaelen resides in [[[[Oakhaven Town|Oakhaven]] Town]], where his presence offers both protection and a deep, unspoken understanding of the world's darker truths. He is a living legend, a man whose story is woven into the fabric of the region.

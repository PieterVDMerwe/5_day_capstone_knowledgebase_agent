# Thorne Frostbrand

Thorne Frostbrand is an ancient Elf, marked by the hardships of his long life; his body is scarred and weathered, a testament to countless battles fought.

As a retired soldier, Thorne has traded the battlefield for the solitude of the [[The Northern Reaches]], a domain that mirrors his own complex nature. His immense age is matched only by his sharp intellect, characterized by an ambitious drive, cunning strategy, and profound wisdom gained from centuries of experience.

His loyalty lies firmly with [[The Bloodrunners]], a faction to whom he owes a debt forged in conflict and allegiance. Thorne now operates within the fringes of this organization, leveraging his cunning to navigate the treacherous political and physical terrain of the North, seeking new ambitions away from the sword and shield.

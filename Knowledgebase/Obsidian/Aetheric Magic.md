Aetheric magic is heavily utilized by Elves. The flow of magic was permanently disrupted during [The Great Sundering]. Objects like the [[Bloodlust Warhammer]] are immune to its effects. [[Oakhaven Town]], The Bloodrunners, and Liam the Blacksmith are also mentioned as known entities in this text.

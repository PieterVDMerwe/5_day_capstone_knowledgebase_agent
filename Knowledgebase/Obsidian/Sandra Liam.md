Sandra Liam is the steadfast and loving wife of Liam the Blacksmith. Known for her kind demeanor and gentle spirit, she is a familiar and cherished face in Oakhaven Town, often seen assisting her husband indirectly or tending to their home. While Liam is at the forge, Sandra ensures the smooth running of their household, creating a comforting sanctuary for them both. Her quiet strength and unwavering support are a cornerstone of Liam's life, allowing him to focus on his demanding work at The Rusty Anvil Tavern. Sandra is well-regarded by the townsfolk for her warmth and practical wisdom.

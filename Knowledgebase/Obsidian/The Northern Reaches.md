# [[The Northern Reaches]]

As a distinct geographical location, [[The Northern Reaches]] is a region defined by its striking tropical climate, which contrasts sharply with any typical northern environment. Despite this warm setting, the area is densely populated, housing approximately 33,000 inhabitants.

Geographically, the Reaches directly borders or encompasses the settlement of [[Oakhaven Town]], making it an integral part of the local landscape. However, the true power within these lands is held not by its inhabitants alone, but by a dominant external force: [[The Bloodrunners]]. This controlling faction exerts absolute authority over the entire region, shaping its history and dictating the fate of every settlement within [[The Northern Reaches]].

History records a complex relationship between the local populace and [[The Bloodrunners]], with the territory serving as a critical nexus for their influence.
